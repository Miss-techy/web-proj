Far far away, behind the word mountains, far from the countries Vokalia and Consonantia, there live the blind texts. It is a paradisematic country, in which roasted parts of sentences fly into your mouth. Even the all-powerful Pointing has no control about the blind texts it is an almost unorthographic life.
